About The Mahi Foundation

The Mahi Foundation is a youth-led, non-political initiative inspired by Allama Iqbal’s symbol of Mahi (the fish): a metaphor for freedom, bold thought, and the courage to roam beyond limits. We run leadership programs, community projects and skill workshops across Pakistan — created by students, for students.
